About Us

Driven by Excellence.
Fueled by Passion.

CertifiedCityWhips was founded with a simple vision: make premium car rentals accessible, transparent, and unforgettable. We believe every journey should feel extraordinary.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, CertifiedCityWhips began as a two-car operation in Beverly Hills. What started as a passion project quickly grew into one of the most trusted names in premium car rental. Today, we operate across 7 major cities with a hand-picked fleet of over 500 vehicles.

Our founder saw a gap in the market — luxury car rentals that didn't come with hidden fees, poor customer service, and outdated vehicles. CCW was built to be the antithesis of traditional car rental: transparent pricing, immaculate vehicles, and a booking experience that takes under 2 minutes.
