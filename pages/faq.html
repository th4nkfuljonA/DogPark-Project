Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting with CertifiedCityWhips.

How do I book a vehicle? +
Browse our fleet, select a vehicle, choose your dates, and complete the checkout process. The entire booking takes less than 2 minutes. You'll receive a confirmation email with pickup instructions immediately.
What are the age requirements? +
You must be at least 21 years old with a valid driver's license held for a minimum of 2 years. For exotic and sports vehicles, the minimum age is 25. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge.
Is insurance included? +
Basic liability coverage is included with every rental. We recommend adding our Comprehensive Coverage ($29/day) or Full Protection Package ($49/day) for complete peace of mind. You may also use your personal auto insurance if it covers rental vehicles.
What is the cancellation policy? +
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled pickup. Cancellations within 24–48 hours receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 24 hours or no-shows are non-refundable. Contact us for exceptions due to emergencies.
What happens if the car breaks down? +
All CCW rentals include 24/7 roadside assistance at no extra cost. In the unlikely event of a mechanical issue, call our hotline and we'll dispatch assistance or a replacement vehicle within 60 minutes in metro areas.
Can I extend my rental? +
Yes! You can extend your rental through your dashboard or by calling us, subject to vehicle availability. Extensions are charged at the same daily rate. We recommend extending at least 12 hours before your scheduled return.
Is there a mileage limit? +
Standard rentals include 200 miles per day. Unlimited mileage packages are available for most vehicles at an additional cost. Exotic vehicles are limited to 100 miles per day with additional miles charged at $2.50/mile.
Do you offer delivery? +
Yes, we offer vehicle delivery and pickup within a 30-mile radius of our locations. Airport delivery is available at select locations. Delivery fees vary by distance, starting at $50. Contact us for a quote.

Still have questions?

Our team is available 24/7. Reach us at hello@certifiedcitywhips.com or call +1 (800) CCW-WHIPS.
